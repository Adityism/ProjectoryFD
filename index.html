Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

What is Projectory?

Projectory is a platform that offers custom-built software projects for students and professionals. We specialize in web apps, AI/ML projects, and more, delivering high-quality code, design, and viva support for project presentations.

What do I get in the ₹599 Standard Tier?

For ₹599/month, you'll receive a standard web app or AI project with clean, well-structured code and one modification after delivery. This tier is perfect for those who need a basic yet professional project at an affordable price.

What do I get in the ₹749 Premium Tier?

In the ₹749/month Premium Tier, you get enhanced UI/UX design, the option to select themes, and two modifications after delivery. Additionally, you receive priority support and access to premium templates, making this ideal for those who need a polished, well-rounded project.

What do I get in the ₹899 Advanced Tier?

The ₹899/month Advanced Tier offers fully customizable UI design, fast-track delivery within 72 hours, and three modifications after delivery. It also includes 24/7 support and expert viva guidance to help you confidently present and explain your project, making it perfect for those aiming for top grades.

Do you provide viva preparation?

Yes, viva preparation is included in the Premium and Advanced tiers. We provide detailed viva support, explaining key talking points, how to present your project, and guiding you through code explanations for maximum confidence during your viva.

How long does it take to complete my project?

• Standard Tier: Typically delivered within 7 days
• Premium Tier: Delivered within 4-6 days
• Advanced Tier: Delivered within 72 hours with fast-track delivery

What if I need my project sooner?

If you require an urgent project, the Advanced Tier includes fast-track delivery in under 72 hours. For other urgent needs, please contact us, and we will try to accommodate your timeline.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with my project?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. We offer revisions based on your tier (1, 2, or unlimited revisions) to ensure the project meets your expectations. Our team works closely with you until you're fully satisfied.
